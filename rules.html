Follow the rules

List of all Minecraft and Discord server rules.

Ignorance of the rules is no excuse!

Minecraft server

  1. Respect the Blocky Code: Respect every player. No discrimination, hate speech, or trolling. Let's keep our community kind and inclusive.
  2. Minecraft Vibes Only: This is a Minecraft-centric space. Discuss builds, adventures, and redstone creations. Let’s keep the conversation blocky!
  3. Chat Etiquette Matters: Be mindful of your words. No spamming, excessive caps, or disruptive behavior. Let’s keep the chat flowing smoothly.
  4. Family-Friendly Environment: Keep it family-friendly. No inappropriate content or language. We're building a community for all ages.
  5. Build Respectful Relations: Collaborate and communicate. If you have a dispute, take it to private messages. Let’s build friendships, not walls.
  6. Mind Your Coordinates: Use the right channels. Survival discussions in the survival channel, creative in creative. Let’s keep it organized for everyone.
  7. Moderators' Word is Final: Listen to our moderators. They're here to ensure fairness and harmony. Respect their decisions.
  8. No X-Ray Vision Allowed: No cheating or exploiting. Play fair and square. We’re here to enjoy the game in its true essence.

Discord server

  1. Respect and Kindness First: Treat fellow members with respect. No hate speech or discrimination. Let’s build a positive and friendly community.
  2. Minecraft Love Only: Keep the chatter focused on Minecraft. Share your experiences, creations, and tips - this is our Minecraft sanctuary!
  3. Keep it Family-Friendly: This is a family-friendly zone. Keep conversations and content suitable for all ages.
  4. Respectful Disagreements: It’s okay to disagree. Keep it civil and respectful. If things heat up, take it to DMs.
  5. No Trolling or Griefing Discussions: Let’s keep it positive. No trolling or discussions about griefing. Focus on the good vibes!
  6. Use Appropriate Channels: Post in the right places. Survival stuff in the survival channel, please. Keep it organized!
  7. Moderator Instructions: Follow our moderators' guidance. They're here to keep the Minecraft vibes strong and positive.
  8. No NSFW Content: Keep it clean. No NSFW content, including profile pics and nicknames. Let’s keep the server safe for all.

If you have read the rules, you can go and enjoy the fun on our server!
